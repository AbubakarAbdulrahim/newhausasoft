About Us

Hausasoft Technologies is a forward-thinking and innovative tech company committed to fostering digital transformation and bridging the gap between technology and cultural relevance. Rooted in a mission to empower communities, Hausasoft specializes in providing a wide range of technology services, including website design and development, culturally aligned content creation, mobile app development, full-stack data science consulting, and comprehensive technical support. Its primary focus is on delivering cutting-edge solutions that cater specifically to the Hausa-speaking community, ensuring that cultural values and local needs are at the forefront of every project. Hausasoft Technologies thrives on principles of inclusivity, simplicity, and a future-oriented approach, making it a trusted partner for individuals, businesses, and organizations navigating the digital era. With an emphasis on cultural preservation and community empowerment, the company not only meets current technological demands but also inspires innovation for generations to come. The vision behind Hausasoft Technologies is brought to life by its founders, Abubakar Abdulrahim and Al-mustapha Umar, both passionate technologists with a shared goal of driving progress through technology. Abubakar Abdulrahim, an accomplished IT professional and cybersecurity analyst certified by Cisco, hails from Kofar Wambai, Kano, Nigeria. Currently a student at Bayero University Kano, Abubakar has a strong background in full-stack web development and a keen interest in leveraging his skills to uplift his community. His leadership, dedication to innovation, and commitment to cultural values are central to Hausasoft’s success. Al-mustapha Umar, the co-founder and CEO of Arabit Collections, brings a wealth of experience and insight to Hausasoft. As a visionary leader, Al-mustapha shares Abubakar’s passion for creating meaningful digital solutions tailored to the unique needs of the Hausa-speaking community. Together, they have built a company that stands as a beacon of technological advancement, focusing on bridging gaps in accessibility, fostering collaboration, and driving impactful change. Through their combined efforts, Hausasoft Technologies has positioned itself as a pioneer in the tech industry, delivering excellence while staying true to its roots.
